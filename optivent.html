AN AWARD WINNING LOGO

OBJECTIVE

To design an innovative logo for the Kamloops Startup Weekend Competition. We had 72 hours to design a brand and prototype and present the final result to a panel of judges. The brand our group created was a ventilation system that you can control the temperatures with your phone.

SOLUTION

I chose the green mint colour to give the logo a "fresh" look. I only used this method for the last four letters to show that the air is blowing in a specific direction.
