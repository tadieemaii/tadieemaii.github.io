About

Working in an agency, has taught me many valuable skills. Such as teamwork, usability testing/user interviews, wire framing, and public speaking. Tools such as Sketch, Invision, and the Adobe Suite are programs I am very confident in using.

Traveling has influenced me in a number of ways. From biking the Gardens of Versailles to exploring the Spanish Forts in San Juan, traveling has really inspired me as a designer. When I am home, I find inspiration through hiking, photography, and yoga.

Design is something I am very passionate about. Being able to do what I love is a blessing. I enjoy understanding the behaviour and creating a visually-appealing experience for the user. I always strive to learn new skills and stay involved in the community.

Business Card Design

OBJECTIVE

To design a single sided business card that give all the TRUSU Computer Science Clubs contact information to students around campus.

SOLUTION

I wanted to design and create an innovative way to display the information as visual, and as large as I possibly can. I used a geometric style layout and incorporated the blue and the green from the logo. The information is on a slant because I wanted the information to align with the crisp geometric edge, thus giving the piece a clean and unified look.
